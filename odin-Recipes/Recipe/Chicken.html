Butter chicken is an Indian dish originating in Delhi. It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste.

Butter chicken curry is a rich, creamy Indian dish featuring tender pieces of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in a tomato-based sauce generously flavored with butter, garlic, ginger, garam masala, and often finished with heavy cream, typically served with rice or naan bread; the name "butter chicken" directly refers to the prominent use of butter in the sauce, giving it a smooth, velvety texture.
